A little about me...
My name is Prisila Simental and i'm from San Francisco. I attend Balboa High school and will be going in as a Junior this incoming year. Some of my important skills and qualifications are not giving up easily, having patience, Bilingual, tolerance to different situations, and open to new ideas.

My experience in Dev/Mission has been an adventure and very new thing that I have ever done. Before this program I absolutely had no idea on how to code or any knowledge about hardware. I am one of the youngest participants and when the other participants over heard my age during the interview they really tried teaching me a lot of things. They took me under their wing and shared their knoledge on coding or Hardware and gave me advice on anything. This program has been very welcoming to every one of us and this group has been comprehensive and flexible with eachothers different knowledge levels of coding.The short amount of time I have knowing them we have become like a family who is united and counts on eachother for anything.

I want to end this program with a quote.
"Change is the end result of all true learning,"
by Leo Buscaglia.
